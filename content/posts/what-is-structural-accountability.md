Public figures speak at length and apparent specificity while committing to almost nothing evaluable. Klaritex measures structural completeness — whether a public commitment contains enough specified information to be evaluated later by a neutral observer.

The six commitment elements — Who, Action, Object, Measure, Timeline, Premise — are the irreducible anatomy of an accountable statement.
